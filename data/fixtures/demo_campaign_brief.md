# Campaign Brief

## Client
GlowNest

## Brand
Clean beauty hair and body care for ingredient-conscious shoppers.

## Objective
Identify the strongest audience tensions and launch risks before scaling a
paid creator campaign.

## Target Audience
Sensitive-skin buyers, clean beauty shoppers, value seekers, and people who
read reviews before trying a new routine product.

## Product Context
The launch focuses on a moisturizing body and hair care line with a light
botanical scent, recyclable packaging, and claims around everyday softness.

## Required Platforms
TikTok, Instagram Reels, Amazon product detail pages.

## Tone Constraints
Plainspoken, practical, transparent. Avoid medical claims, miracle language,
or vague phrases like "safe for everyone."

## Claims To Avoid
Do not claim the product prevents irritation, treats skin conditions, or works
for every scent preference.

## Campaign Questions
- Which objections should creators address directly?
- Which benefits have credible review language behind them?
- Which risks should the paid-media team watch before increasing spend?
